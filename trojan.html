What is Trojan?

The **Trojan protocol** is an advanced proxy technology used to provide **secure and encrypted internet access**. It is popular for its **performance and stealth capabilities**, making it useful for bypassing censorship and firewalls by masquerading as normal HTTPS traffic.

Key Features

Secure and Fast

Trojan provides strong encryption and high-speed connections, ensuring your data is protected without sacrificing performance.

Stealth

Using HTTPS, Trojan blends in with regular web traffic, making it incredibly difficult for network firewalls to detect and block.

Reliable

Enjoy stable connections even in areas with high network congestion, thanks to its robust design and efficient protocol.

Cross-platform

Works seamlessly across a wide range of devices and operating systems, so you can stay protected no matter what you're using.

The Trojan protocol offers a perfect balance of **security, speed, and reliability**, making it a prime choice for users seeking privacy and internet freedom.
